$\gdef\T#1{{#1^\top}}$
$\gdef\vx{\vec{x}}$
$\gdef\vb{\vec{b}}$
$\gdef\vc{\vec{c}}$

## Introduction

Conjugate gradient has been used for:
- Solving a sparse linear system of equations $A\vx=b$ where $A \in \R^{n
\times n}$, symmetric and positive definite.
- Minimizing a quadratic function $f(x) = \frac{1}{2}\T{\vx}A\vx - \T{\vb}\vx + \vc$
- Minimizing a function $f: \R^n \rightarrow \R$.

## Important notation
- Error and residual do not point the same direction.
